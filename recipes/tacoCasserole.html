Taco Casserole

A fun, layered dish that combines Mexican flavors in a beginner-friendly way

Ingridients
  • Ground beef
  • onio
  • taco seasoning
  • black beans
  • Corn
  • salsa
  • tortilla chips
  • Mexican cheese
Ordered to do list
  1. pre heat oven to 180 degreese
  2. cook ground beef in a skillet until browned
  3. add onion and sauce until soften
  4. stir in taco seasoning, black beans, corn and salsa
  5. laeyer tortilla chips in the bottom of baking dish
  6. spread beef mixture evenly over the chips
  7. top with shredded cheese
  8. bake uncovered for 20-25 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly
  9. cool slightly before serving
