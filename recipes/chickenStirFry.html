Chicke Stir Fry

A quick, colorful dish that teaches you how to balance protein, vegetables, and sauce.

Ingridients:
  • Chicken thighs
  • Rice noodles
  • Soy sauce
  • Red bell pepper
  • snow peas
  • green onion
  • Sesame oil
Ordered to do list:
  1. Slice chicken into thin stripes
  2. chop vegetable
  3. heat oil
  4. add vegetables to the work and stir fry for 3-4 minutes until crisp
  5. return chicken to the pan
  6. Pour in sauce
  7. Stir fry everything togather for 2-3 minutes
  8. serve hot
